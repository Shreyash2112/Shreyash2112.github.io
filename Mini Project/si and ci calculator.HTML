SIMPLE INTEREST & COMPOUND INTEREST CALCULATOR

You calculate the simple interest by multiplying the principal amount by the number of periods and the interest rate. Simple interest does not compound, and you don't have to pay interest on interest. Compared to compound interest, simple interest is easier to calculate and easier to understand. ... When it comes to investing, compound interest is better since it allows funds to grow at a faster rate than they would in an account with a simple interest rate.
